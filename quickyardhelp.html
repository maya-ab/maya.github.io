Quick Yard Help


Quick Yard Help was created to connect individuals in need of yard services, like lawn mowing, snow removal, or general outdoor maintenance, with neighbors willing to lend a hand. This service is particularly valuable for seniors or those with limited mobility. This app offers an in-app messaging service to allow users to sort out the details of the arrangement.


My Role


  • Developed the app from concept to prototype by gathering requirements, defining core functionality, user flow, and design.
  • Implemented front-end and back-end components to create a functional prototype.



Features


  • Sign up as either a service provider or a service requester
  • View yard service providers near you
  • Request services from yard service providers
  • Message with your yard service provider
  • Get updated instantly when your yard service provider shows up
