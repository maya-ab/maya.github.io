GDP & CO2 Data Analysis


This project was created as part of a project for my data analytics course. We worked in teams of three, but each had our own research questions. Here I'm displaying only my section of the research, which focused on CO2 emissions and GDP. My research questions were:

  • How does the relationship between GDP and CO2 emissions compare for the 15 highest income countries vs the 15 lowest income countries over recent years?

  • How does the average CO2 emissions per capita of high GDP countries compare to low GDP countries, and the rest of the world?
