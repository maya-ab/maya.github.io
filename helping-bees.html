Helping Bees


Helping Bees is an app that aims to make it easier for people to find volunteer opportunities. The app is something I started alongside three other friends as part of a club competition project. We still continue to work on it in our free time!


My Role


When we first started working on the app, I worked on pitch presentations and creating infographics (such as the venn diagram). Once the competition concluded, my role shifted to focusing more on the development of the app.
